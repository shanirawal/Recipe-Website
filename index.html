Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup milk (warm, as needed to form dough)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ghee or oil (for deep frying)

Recipe

  1. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar, water, and cardamom pods until
    sugar dissolves, add lemon juice, saffron, and rose water, then keep warm.
  2. Make Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda,
    cardamom powder, and ghee, then gradually add warm milk to form a soft dough.
  3. Shape Balls: Divide dough into small smooth balls without cracks.
  4. Fry Gulab Jamuns: Heat ghee or oil on low heat, fry balls until golden brown, stirring gently.
  5. Soak in Syrup: Add hot jamuns to warm sugar syrup and let them soak for at least 1-2 hours.
  6. Serve: Garnish with chopped nuts and serve warm or chilled.

Ras Malai

Ingredients:

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornflour (optional, for binding)
  • Sugar Syrup
  • Rabri

Recipe

  1. Prepare Chhena (Paneer): Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle it, strain, rinse, and knead until smooth.
  2. Make Rasmalai Discs: Shape chhena into small flat discs.
  3. Prepare Sugar Syrup: Boil water and sugar, then add chhena discs and cook until they double in size.
  4. Prepare Rabri (Milk Syrup): Boil milk until reduced, add sugar, saffron, cardamom, and chopped nuts.
  5. Assemble: Gently squeeze excess syrup from chhena discs and soak them in the rabri.
  6. Chill & Serve: Refrigerate for a few hours, garnish with nuts, and serve cold.
